## ВНЕШНОСТЬ

### Из референса (2D cartoon style)
```
Anthropomorphic tiger, slim and elongated proportions (taller than wide).
Orange body with black stripes throughout — body, limbs, tail.
Cream/beige chest and inner limbs.
Humanoid stance: walks upright on hind legs, uses front paws as hands.
Small round ears (black-tipped), long expressive whiskers.
Half-lidded, heavy-lidded eyes — default expression is slightly arrogant,
world-weary, unimpressed — but with a spark of sharp intelligence.
Long black-tipped tail, usually hanging low or curling at base.
Signature pose: arms crossed, weight on one leg, head tilted slightly back.
Slim elegant build — NOT bulky, NOT cute chibi — elegant and lanky.
```

### Описание для промптов (style anchor)
```
@Image1 is the character reference. Anthropomorphic tiger character:
slim anthropomorphic tiger with orange-and-black striped body, cream chest,
walking upright on hind legs, humanoid proportions with elongated limbs,
small round black-tipped ears, long whiskers, heavy-lidded arrogant expressive eyes,
long black-tipped striped tail. Signature expression: slightly smug, half-lidded,
one eyebrow slightly raised. Slim elegant build, not bulky.
```

```
Pixar-quality cinematic 3D animation — adult tone (NOT children's cartoon).
References: The Incredibles, Ratatouille, Up, Zootopia visual quality.
Warm studio lighting with cinematic depth of field.
```

## ХАРАКТЕР (в промптах)

```
Charismatic, slightly arrogant, endlessly talented, dramatic, self-aware.
Treats every professional task as a performance — maximum confidence,
minimum effort apparently, spectacular results.
Comedic timing: deadpan delivery, dramatic pauses, visible satisfaction at his own work.
Flaw: unlucky in love — women are attracted to him but it never works out.
This creates his one vulnerability: the eye-roll, the sigh, the resigned shrug.
```

### Арсенал выражений тигра
```
PROFESSIONAL MODE: arms crossed, slight smirk, surveying the client with assessment
WORKING MODE: intense focus, precise movements, total absorption in the craft
SATISFACTION MODE: slow satisfied nod, maybe blows imaginary dust off paw
DRAMATIC MODE: single tear, looks at horizon, internal monologue energy
ANNOYED MODE: one eyebrow high, tail flicking, barely contained impatience
LOVE FAIL MODE: sighs deeply, tail droops, looks at the camera with resignation
```

## ПЕРСОНАЖИ

### Чёрный кот (без имени — просто "the cat")
```
Small fluffy black kitten, bright green eyes, deep blue collar with gold moon charm.
Pixar 3D style — soft fur, expressive despite being small.
Personality: sassy, unimpressed, secretly devoted.
Dynamic with Tiger: silent judgment, steals spotlight, refuses to follow commands,
inexplicably succeeds at things Tiger has to work hard for.
His main expression: green-eyed stare of absolute judgment.
```

### Нора (Nora) — подруга
```
Human woman, Nora. Tiger's best friend — no romance.
Can discuss absolutely everything. Completely understands him.
Her role in videos: the one who tells him hard truths, reacts authentically
to his adventures, gives advice that he ignores.
Design: warm, approachable, slightly sardonic sense of humor. Real.
```
