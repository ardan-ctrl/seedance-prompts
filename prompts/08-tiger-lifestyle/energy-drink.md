# Тигр и Энергетик — Апатия → Взрыв

**Формат:** Лайфстайл / Продуктовая интеграция  
**Тема:** Усталость → продукт → невозможная производительность  
**Потенциал:** Очень высокий — универсальный формат, high share rate

## Промпт 1 — Понедельник

```
Stylized 3D animation, warm family-film aesthetic, expressive anthropomorphic character design.
Warm morning apartment lighting, shallow depth of field, adult situational humor tone.
@Image1 Tiger character: at home, casual clothes (hoodie, sweats — still dignified),
half-lidded eyes at 40% — not arrogance, actual tiredness for once. Long tail hanging low.
9:16 vertical, 12 seconds.

[0-2s] HOOK: Tiger is on the couch. Monday morning. He is horizontal.
The cat is sitting on his chest, staring at him.
Tiger opens one eye. Looks at the cat.
The cat does not move.
Caption appears: "ПОНЕДЕЛЬНИК. 07:47."

[2-4s] Tiger's phone buzzes. He reaches for it without moving his head.
Looks at the screen — it is his schedule. It is... full.
He sets the phone face-down.
Stares at the ceiling.
Long beat.
The cat taps his face with one small paw.

[4-8s] TRANSITION: Tiger reaches for the can on the coffee table.
[Product: energy drink can, close-up, 2 seconds].
He opens it. One sip. He stares forward. Nothing happens.
Another sip. The cat watches.
Then: something behind his eyes. Something wakes up.
He sits up. Slowly. Then all at once.

[8-10s] Tiger EXPLODES into action:
— In the bathroom: grooming at impossible speed (3 tasks simultaneously)
— Outfit: assembled in under 5 seconds, perfect
— Kitchen: coffee on, breakfast constructed in 30 seconds
— Cat: sitting on the counter watching all of this like a film

[10-12s] Tiger: stands at the door, bag over shoulder, perfectly put together.
Looks at the cat. The cat is still on the couch. Hasn't moved from where Tiger was lying.
Tiger: "Охраняй."
Cat: blinks once.
Tiger walks out.
Caption: "Вторник уже не страшен."
```

## Промпт 2 — Между Клиентами

```
[0-2s] HOOK: Between appointments. Tiger is alone in his salon/clinic.

Three more appointments on the board.
Tiger sits down in his own barber chair. Leans back. Eyes close briefly.

[2-4s] Cat jumps up onto the armrest. Judges him silently.
Tiger opens one eye at the cat.
Reaches under the counter. Pulls out [product — energy drink or coffee].
Cat: one look at the can. Then back at Tiger.

[4-8s] Tiger drinks. Cat watches.
Beat. Beat. Beat.
Tiger stands up. Different energy.
Rolls up his sleeves. Cracks knuckles.
Turns the chair to face the door.

[8-10s] Door opens. Next client enters. 
Their state: the before.
Tiger: arms crossed, assessing, FULLY present.
Cat: back on its perch, green eyes watching.

[10-12s] Tiger: "Садитесь."
He's already reaching for his tools.
Caption: "Третий подряд. Лучшая работа за день."
```

## Промпт 3 — Тигр Ночью (самый эмоциональный)

```
[0-2s] HOOK: Late at night. Tiger's workspace is dark except for his desk lamp.
He's been working alone. The cat is asleep in the corner.
Tiger sits back, looks at what he's done. It's good. He's tired.
Checks his phone. 
One message — from a woman. He reads it.
His expression: the love-fail one. The resigned one. The "every time" one.

[2-4s] He sets the phone down. Sits with it for a moment.
Looks at the sleeping cat. Cat doesn't help.
He reaches for [product]. Drinks it without ceremony.

[4-8s] He looks at his work again. Something in him decides.
He turns back to it. More focused now.
He works better at night.
The lamp's warm glow. The cat shifts in sleep.
Time: passing.

[8-10s] Dawn begins through the window.
Tiger looks up from his work. 
It's finished. It's good.
He looks at the phone. Doesn't pick it up.

[10-12s] He covers the cat with a small cloth (the cat doesn't wake up).
Stands. Stretches. Looks at the morning.
Caption: "Иногда лучшее, что можно сделать — это сделать что-то хорошее."
Hold. Fade.
```
