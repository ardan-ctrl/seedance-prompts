# Тигр-Тату-Мастер

**Профессия:** Тату-мастер  
**Вирусный потенциал:** ★★★★★ (Tier 1 — #3 по исследованию)  
**Крючок:** Клиент приносит референс — нарисованный ручкой на салфетке

---

## Промпт 1 — Референс с Салфетки

```
Stylized 3D animation, warm family-film aesthetic, expressive anthropomorphic character design.
Warm tattoo studio lighting (Edison bulbs), shallow depth of field.
@Image1 Tiger character: wearing black apron, sleeves rolled up showing own striped fur pattern,
black latex gloves, tattoo machine in hand. Half-lidded arrogant golden eyes, professional focus.
9:16 vertical, 15 seconds.

[0-2s] HOOK: Client slides a piece of paper across the desk.
Tiger looks at it. It's a stick figure with a star.
It was drawn in blue ballpoint pen, on a napkin, with a mark from a coffee cup.
Client: "Вот что я хочу."
Tiger looks at the napkin. 
Looks at the client.
Looks at the napkin.
Picks it up very carefully. Examines it.

[2-4s] Tiger looks at the client for a long moment.
Looks at the canvas they're offering (their arm).
Something shifts in his eyes — not defeat. Translation problem accepted.
He sets the napkin down. Opens his tablet. Begins to draw.
What he draws: not the stick figure. But what the stick figure wanted to be.

[4-11s] WORK MONTAGE — tattoo as translation:
— Tiger's tablet sketch: the concept behind the stick figure, elevated
— Client sees the sketch. Eyes go wide. "Это... да. Именно это."
— Tiger nods once. Begins setting up the machine.
— Close-up on the machine moving — Tiger's control is absolute
— Client's grip on the armrest: progressively loosening as trust builds
— Tiger's tail: steady. He's in his zone.
— The tiger fur stripe pattern on his arm: almost functions as a template for precision

[11-13s] REVEAL: The finished piece. Camera holds on the tattoo.
It is beautiful. Technically impeccable. Emotionally precise.
Client stares at their own arm.
Looks up at Tiger. Cannot speak.

[13-15s] Tiger: puts down the machine. Starts cleaning up.
Doesn't look for the compliment. Already done.
Client takes a photo. Immediately posts it.
Caption on the phone: "Нашёл своего мастера."
Tiger: already greeting the next client.
```

## Промпт 2 — Перекрытие Катастрофы

```
[0-2s] HOOK: Client shows their existing tattoo.
It was... ambitious. The execution: less so.
Words are misspelled. The proportions of the face-portrait are experimental.
The shading: visible but confused.
Client: "Можешь что-то сделать?"
Tiger studies it. Tilts his head once. Other direction.
"Сколько времени займёт?"
Tiger: "Сколько нужно."

[2-4s] Tiger examines the old piece for longer than comfortable.
Then: opens his sketchbook. Begins immediately. No hesitation.
The new design incorporates — rather than erases — the old marks.
Client watches the sketch appear: what was a mistake becomes a shadow, a texture, a story.

[4-11s] WORK MONTAGE — cover-up as revelation:
— Layer by layer, the old tattoo transforms
— Tiger works in silence, no unnecessary movement
— The cat appears on the counter (uninvited), watches the process with great interest
— Tiger glances at the cat: one look that means "go away"
— Cat stays. Tiger continues. This is their dynamic.
— The new piece builds: the old errors becoming deliberate elements

[11-13s] REVEAL: The cover-up is complete. What was a mistake is now integrated.
The new tattoo is better FOR having the old one underneath.
Client holds very still. Mouth slightly open.
"Оно... стало частью?"
Tiger: without looking up from cleaning tools. "Оно всегда было частью."

[13-15s] TWIST: Client examines it. Looks at Tiger.
"А у тебя есть татуировки?"
Tiger: pauses. Looks at his own stripes.
Both look at his striped arm simultaneously.
Tiger: puts on his jacket.
"Следующий."
```
