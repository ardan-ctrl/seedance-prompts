# Тигр-Личный Тренер

**Профессия:** Личный тренер  
**Вирусный потенциал:** ★★★★★ (Tier 1 — #2 по исследованию)  
**Твист:** Тигр смотрит на своё отражение во время тренировки клиента

---

## Промпт 1 — Абсолютный Новичок

```
Stylized 3D animation, warm family-film aesthetic, expressive anthropomorphic character design.
Warm gym lighting, shallow depth of field, adult situational humor tone.
@Image1 Tiger character: slim anthropomorphic tiger in fitted black tracksuit
with orange accent stripe, stopwatch around neck, arms crossed authority pose.
Orange-and-black striped fur, half-lidded arrogant golden eyes.
9:16 vertical, 15 seconds.

[0-2s] HOOK: Medium shot — the client arrives at the gym. Anthropomorphic bear
(soft-bodied, friendly, wearing a new expensive fitness outfit — still with tags on).
He looks at the equipment like ancient alien technology.
He looks at the Tiger. The Tiger looks at him.
Tiger: uncrosses arms. Picks up clipboard.
Makes a note.

[2-4s] Tiger shows him a pushup. Demonstrates one with perfect form.
He does it so effortlessly it looks insulting.
Then: steps back. Gestures toward the floor.
Client attempts the pushup.
It goes... poorly.

[4-11s] TRAINING MONTAGE — time passes:
— Tiger with stopwatch, arms crossed, watching client on equipment
— The bear struggles with the most basic movements
— Tiger makes adjustments: moves client's arm, corrects posture — one precise touch each time
— Weeks passing (visual cue: Bear getting marginally, then noticeably better)
— Tiger watching his own reflection in the gym mirror ONCE — nods at himself approvingly
— Client catches him doing this. Tiger doesn't acknowledge it.
— Final session: bear stands at the barbell. More weight than he imagined.
— Tiger: one nod.

[11-13s] REVEAL: The bear lifts it. Clean form. Sets it down.
Turns around. Different person.
Tiger makes a final note on clipboard. 
Turns it around. It says only: "Готов."

[13-15s] TWIST: Bear immediately puts on the expensive new fitness outfit he wore on day one.
Poses for a mirror photo. The outfit fits completely differently now.
Tiger looks at the photo over his shoulder.
Their eyes meet in the phone screen reflection.
Tiger: one very slow nod.
Caption: "Зато теги сохранил."
```

## Промпт 2 — Нарцисс в Зале

```
[0-2s] HOOK: An extremely fit anthropomorphic leopard arrives.
He is already in incredible shape. He knows it.
He flexes for the mirror before acknowledging Tiger.
Tiger watches this for three seconds.
"Зачем ты здесь?"
Leopard: "Хочу быть лучше."
Tiger: "Лучше — это не про форму."
Leopard blinks. This was unexpected.

[2-4s] Tiger does not show him any equipment.
He shows him a balance board. And asks him to simply stand on it.
Leopard: confident. Immediately falls off.
Tiger: did not smile. But his tail moves once.

[4-11s] WORK MONTAGE — precision training:
— Balance, coordination, functional strength
— Leopard falls off several things (each with increasing dignity)
— Tiger adjusts, corrects, challenges — never laughs, never praises cheaply
— Leopard stops looking at himself in the mirror
— He starts looking at what he's doing instead
— Final session: the coordination exercise that defeated him on day one — he completes it

[11-13s] REVEAL: Leopard stands differently. Moves differently.
He walks past the mirror. He glances. Moves on.
Tiger: closes clipboard.

[13-15s] Leopard: "Я даже лучше не стал."
Tiger: "Нет. Стал."
Long beat.
Tiger: already walking away. "Следующий."
Caption: "Форма — это последнее, что меняется."
```

## Промпт 3 — Его Собственная Тренировка (Тигр ОДИН)

```
[0-2s] HOOK: The gym is empty. Early morning. Tiger is here alone.
He stands at the mirror. Not posing. Just looking. Honest moment.
He rolls his neck. Straightens. Puts in earbuds. His tail stills.

[2-4s] He begins.

[4-11s] TRAINING MONTAGE — Tiger working alone:
— He is extraordinary. Every movement perfect, efficient, almost art.
— But he's doing this alone. No audience. No client to impress.
— The cat arrives (snuck in), sits on a weight bench, watching.
— Tiger sees the cat in the mirror. Doesn't stop.
— Cat: green eyes, judging nothing in particular. Or everything.
— Tiger works harder.

[11-13s] Tiger finishes. Stands at the mirror. Breathing steadily.
He looks good. He knows he looks good.
He looks at the cat.
Cat: blinks slowly. Once.

[13-15s] Tiger: dries his face with a towel. Looks at his phone.
One unread message. He looks at it.
The expression: the love-fail face. The resigned one.
He pockets the phone.
Picks up his bag. Walks toward the door.
Caption: "Понедельник."
```
