# Тигр-Портной

**Профессия:** Портной / Стилист  
**Вирусный потенциал:** ★★★★★ (Tier 1 — #5 по исследованию)  
**Крючок:** Костюм куплен на 3 размера больше, бабушка "подшила"

---

## Промпт 1 — Спасение Костюма

```
Pixar-quality cinematic 3D animation, adult Pixar aesthetic (Zootopia/Incredibles tone),
warm atelier lighting, shallow depth of field, rich amber color grading.
@Image1: slim anthropomorphic tiger, elegant three-piece suit (his own, impeccable),
measuring tape around neck, pincushion on wrist, slim reading glasses for close work.
Orange-and-black striped, walks upright, heavy-lidded arrogant eyes.
Maintain exact character appearance throughout. 9:16 vertical, 15 seconds.

[0-2s] HOOK: Medium shot on the client — a man in a suit so wrong it defies physics.
The jacket hits mid-thigh, the sleeves extend past his fingertips, the shoulders
are somewhere around his ears. He checks his watch (under the sleeve — can't find it).
He looks up at Tiger, genuinely confident.
"Мне кажется, она немного мешковатый. Можешь подровнять?"
Tiger's left eye twitches. Just once.

[2-4s] Tiger walks a slow circle around the client. Full assessment.
He crouches down to examine where the trouser hem has been folded up four times.
He stands. He removes his own glasses. Pinches the bridge of his nose.
He puts them back on. Accepts his fate.

[4-11s] WORK MONTAGE — tailoring as combat:
— Tiger's chalk marks the client at lightning speed, moving around him like a shark
— Scissors cutting — fast, certain, each motion deliberate
— Pins flying from his mouth as he secures seams (too fast to count)
— The suit jacket on a mannequin: Tiger reconstructs the entire lining
— Needle and thread: his paws move in a blur, the seams invisible
— Steam press — a hiss of steam, the fabric relaxing into perfection
— Tiger stands back, tilts his head, makes one final adjustment

[11-13s] REVEAL: Tiger turns the client around to the mirror.
The same suit. Transformed. Structural, sharp, precisely fit.
The client stares. The man in the mirror is a different person.
He straightens up slightly, as if the suit demanded it.

[13-15s] TWIST: Client nods, satisfied. Starts walking out.
Tiger watches him go. The client puts on his coat.
The coat is the same size as the original suit was.
Tiger slowly closes his eyes.
Caption: "Следующий."
```

## Промпт 2 — "Я Сам Шью"

```
[0-2s] HOOK: A woman enters, wearing a dress she clearly made herself.
The fabric: beautiful. The construction: brave. The seams: visible from orbit.
One sleeve is slightly different length than the other. She is very proud.
Caption: "Я занимаюсь этим три месяца."

[2-4s] Tiger approaches. Examines the dress. Examines one seam.
Holds the seam gently with two claws. It doesn't hold. He looks up.
There is respect in his eyes. Also: a professional emergency.

[4-11s] WORK MONTAGE — tiger unpicks and reconstructs:
— Seam ripper: gentle but decisive, the old stitching removed
— Tiger lays the fabric pieces flat, examines the cut — the shape is actually good
— Reconstruction begins: this time, the seams are invisible
— The dress rebuilt around her, still the same design, now structural
— Final detail: Tiger adds one subtle flourish the client didn't have — a small pleat
— Steam press, the fabric transforms

[11-13s] REVEAL: Same dress. Same design. Completely different garment.
She turns in it once. Looks in the mirror. Runs her hands down the seams.
"Это... это же моё платье?"
Tiger: "Теперь — да."

[13-15s] She takes a photo for Instagram immediately.
Caption: "Сшила сама ✨"
Tiger looks at the camera.
Tiger says nothing.
His tail speaks for him.
```

## Промпт 3 — Жених за Неделю до Свадьбы

```
[0-2s] HOOK: A man walks in with a garment bag. Opens it.
The suit is from 2010. It fit him in 2010.
He holds it up. He holds it against himself.
It is optimistic about 2010 him.
Tiger stares at it. Then at the man. Then at the suit.
"Свадьба когда?"
Man: "В субботу."

[2-4s] Tiger hangs up the suit. Walks around it slowly.
Touches the fabric. The fabric was once good.
Looks at the man. Looks at the suit. Makes a decision.
He reaches for his scissors.

[4-11s] WORK MONTAGE — emergency reconstruction:
— The suit deconstructed and rebuilt with emergency precision
— New lining, reshaped shoulders, the jacket transformed
— Trousers taken in along every seam simultaneously (Tiger works both at once)
— A slight modernization of the lapel — one small cut
— Steam and pressing, the fabric responding
— Tiger checks the clock once during this — nods, on schedule

[11-13s] REVEAL: The man in the suit. Same suit. Different decade.
The 2010 suit now looks like it was made this year, for this man, for this moment.
He stands straighter. He looks like someone who deserves a wedding.

[13-15s] He turns to Tiger. Eyes a little wet.
"Она не видела этого костюма."
Tiger: straightens his lapel for him. Steps back.
"Теперь она увидит."
Man walks out. Tiger watches the door close.
Small moment.
His tail curls once. Quietly.
Caption: "Бывает."
```
