# Тигр-Механик

**Профессия:** Автомеханик  
**Вирусный потенциал:** ★★★★ (Tier 2 — #11 по исследованию)  
**Крючок:** Машина — это больше ржавчина, чем металл

## Промпт 1 — Реанимация

```
Pixar-quality cinematic 3D animation, adult Pixar aesthetic (Zootopia/Incredibles tone),
warm industrial garage lighting (overhead fluorescents + work lamp), shallow depth of field.
@Image1: slim anthropomorphic tiger in grey mechanic's coveralls (open at top, tied at waist),
oil stains on forearms, large wrench in one hand. Orange-and-black striped, upright on hind legs.
Maintain exact character appearance throughout. 9:16 vertical, 15 seconds.

[0-2s] HOOK: A car is towed in on a flatbed. The camera slowly pans it.
Rust patterns cover 60% of the exterior. One door is a different color.
The windshield has a crack running completely across it.
The engine hood is held down with bungee cord.
The owner jumps down from the tow truck cab — proud. Points at it.
"Ей 23 года. Родная. Не продам."
Tiger circles it once. Slowly. Professionally.
He crouches to look underneath. Stands up. 

[2-4s] Tiger places both paws on the car roof (carefully — it flexes).
Something in his expression shifts: not dread. Challenge.
He reaches through the window (the glass is absent) and pops the hood.
The bungee cord snaps back. He doesn't flinch.
He looks at the engine.
He looks at his wrench.
He looks at the camera.
He begins.

[4-11s] WORK MONTAGE — restoration as rebirth:
— Tiger underneath the car, sparks flying from somewhere
— Close-up: his paws move over the engine with a surgeon's precision
— Dents beaten out with perfect force — each hit exactly right
— New panels going on, the rust zones disappearing
— The engine bay cleaned and detailed — parts gleaming
— Paint goes on in rapid strokes — the color chosen: original red
— Tiger's tail moves methodically — he is in his zone
— One final torque of a bolt. Click. Right.

[11-13s] REVEAL: The garage door opens. The car rolls out.
Not new. Better than new. Every original detail preserved,
every problem eliminated. It looks like it's about to enter a concours.
The owner stands in the light. His hands are over his mouth.

[13-15s] He walks to the car. Touches the door handle.
The door opens smoothly, silently, for the first time in years.
He looks at Tiger.
He can't speak.
Tiger: wipes his hands on a rag. Tosses it over his shoulder.
"Она тебя ждала."
Caption: "23 года. Это не старость. Это характер."
```

## Промпт 2 — Машина После Мужа

```
[0-2s] HOOK: A woman arrives. Her car: someone has done DIY modifications to it.
Rearview mirror taped on. A decorative bumper sticker installed crookedly.
Tire pressure looks experimental. The stereo is an afterthought.
Tiger walks around it with a clipboard.
"Муж чинил сам?"
Woman: [very long exhale] "Да."
Tiger: [makes a note] "Понятно."

[2-4s] Tiger opens the hood. Takes a photo of the engine.
Sends it to someone (unknown). Gets a response immediately.
Looks at the engine again. 
Whatever the response said, Tiger sets the phone face-down.

[4-11s] WORK MONTAGE — systematic correction:
— Every DIY modification being professionally redone
— The mirror: properly mounted, aligned
— The stereo: properly integrated
— Engine: each rogue component identified and replaced
— One very specific bolt that was installed backwards
— Tiger holds it up to the light. Sets it down gently. It is not worth discussing.

[11-13s] REVEAL: The car functions as originally designed.
Everything works. The woman sits in the driver's seat and tries everything.
Windows. Lights. Heated seat. Each one: perfect.

[13-15s] She starts the engine. Listens. 
Looks at Tiger through the windshield.
"Он говорил, что хорошо разбирается в машинах."
Tiger: leans on the hood, arms crossed.
"Я знаю."
Caption: "Не все мужчины одинаковы."
```
