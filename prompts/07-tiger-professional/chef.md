# Тигр-Шеф-Повар

**Профессия:** Шеф-повар  
**Формат:** Профессиональная трансформация / кулинарный перфоманс  
**Потенциал:** Очень высокий — формат "трансформация блюда" визуально захватывает

---

## Промпт 1 — Студент с пустым холодильником

```
Pixar-quality cinematic 3D animation, adult Pixar aesthetic (Zootopia/Ratatouille tone),
warm kitchen lighting, shallow depth of field, professional culinary environment.
Main character (@Image1): slim anthropomorphic tiger in white chef's toque and double-breasted
chef coat, orange-and-black striped, upright on hind legs, precise confident movements.
9:16 vertical, 15 seconds.

[0-2s] HOOK: A fridge opens. Inside: one egg, one nearly empty ketchup bottle, a sad wilting
sprig of dill, and a single forgotten carrot in the corner. We see this in full close-up.
Pull back: a hopeful student is presenting this to the Tiger Chef.
"Это всё что есть. Можешь что-то сделать?"
Tiger stares at the fridge. One beat. 

[2-4s] Tiger picks up the egg. Holds it up to the light, examining it.
Looks at the ketchup. Back at the egg. Something in his eyes shifts.
He is not deterred. He is INSPIRED.
He rolls up his sleeves. His tail straightens.

[4-11s] WORK MONTAGE — rapid cuts, kinetic cooking energy:
— The egg cracks perfectly, one-handed, into a hot pan: immediate golden sizzle
— The carrot becomes paper-thin ribbons under the knife (blade barely visible, just motion)
— Close-up: ketchup reduced with something into a complex glossy sauce — impossible
— Tiger's paws move like music, multiple elements in progress simultaneously
— A single flame leaps dramatically (he controls it exactly)
— Steam rises, Tiger tastes, adds a pinch of salt from height
— His tail moves — keeping time with something only he hears
— Student watches with mouth open

[11-13s] REVEAL: Tiger steps back from the pass.
On the plate: an impossibly composed dish. The egg: perfect, architectural.
The ketchup: a complex, glossy sauce drizzled in arcs. The carrot: a delicate garnish.
It looks like a €200 restaurant plate.

[13-15s] The student stares at the plate. Looks up at Tiger.
Looks back at the plate. Carefully, reverently picks up a fork.
Takes one bite. Eyes roll back.
Tiger: already untying his apron. "Следующий."
```

## Промпт 2 — Самоуверенный блогер

```
[0-2s] HOOK: A food blogger enters, pointing at camera confidently.
"Я умею готовить, меня учить не надо."
He shows his latest dish: a pan of pasta glued into one mass,
tomato sauce the color of old paint, burned bottom visible.
He seems proud of this.

[2-4s] Tiger looks at the pan. Slowly takes it from him.
Holds it at arm's length, examining.
Sets it on the counter. Does not throw it away.
He will WORK WITH THIS.

[4-11s] WORK MONTAGE — rescue operation:
— Tiger separates the glued pasta strand by strand (exaggerated precision)
— Builds the sauce from scratch, the pasta pan now a base ingredient
— Fast knife work, aromatics flying, pan flipping with one paw
— Blogger tries to help — Tiger's paw gently pushes him aside without looking
— Multiple elements assembled with impossible speed and control

[11-13s] REVEAL: The same ingredients. Transformed completely.
The blogger stares. "Это... это же моя паста?"
Tiger: "Это была твоя паста."

[13-15s] Blogger takes a photo of it. Opens his Instagram. Posts it.
Immediately gets 10,000 likes. He looks at Tiger.
Tiger: is already gone. 
Caption visible: "#homecooking"
```

## Промпт 3 — Ребёнок не хочет есть овощи

```
[0-2s] HOOK: A child sits at the table. In front of them: broccoli.
The child looks at the broccoli. The broccoli looks back (metaphorically).
The child's expression: absolute refusal. War.
Tiger Chef arrives, surveying the situation.

[2-4s] Tiger picks up a single piece of broccoli. Examines it like a gemstone.
"Значит, так."
Rolls up his sleeves. The child watches, suspicious.

[4-11s] WORK MONTAGE — culinary magic:
— Broccoli transformed: roasted, sauced, composed into something beautiful
— Tiger sculpts the food into a tiny dragon shape on the plate
— Multiple elements added — cheese, crispy textures, the child leans in slowly
— Tiger creates a tiny "volcano" with the broccoli

[11-13s] REVEAL: A plate that looks like an edible adventure game.
The broccoli is unrecognizable. The child stares.
Picks up a fork. Takes a bite. Pause.
Looks at the broccoli. Looks at Tiger. Takes another bite.

[13-15s] Child turns to their parent: "Я хочу это каждый день."
Tiger to camera: one very small satisfied nod.
His tail curls once: satisfied.
```
