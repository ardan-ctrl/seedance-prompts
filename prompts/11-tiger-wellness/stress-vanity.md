# Tiger — Stress & Vanity (Comedy-Emotional Arc)

> "Стресс — это атака на мою внешность. Я отвечаю."

---

## Промпт 1 — Обнаружение (The Wrinkle)

**Сюжет:** Тигр замечает мельчайшую линию на своём лице. Экзистенциальный кризис.

```
Stylized 3D animation, warm family-film aesthetic.
@Image1 as Tiger. Location: bathroom, morning. Close-up lighting, vanity mirror.
9:16 vertical, 15 seconds.

[0-2s] HOOK: Tiger's morning routine. He leans toward the bathroom mirror.
Studies his face. Something catches his eye.
He leans closer. His expression shifts — almost imperceptibly, but we see it.

[2-5s] Tiger examining what appears to be an absolutely microscopic line
near one eye. He turns his face. Different angle. 
Tilts his head. Studies it like a document he must interpret carefully.

[5-9s] Tiger: "Стресс."
He says this quietly. Like a diagnosis.
He straightens up. Looks at himself in full.
"Нет."

[9-12s] He opens a cabinet. His wellness collection:
Supplements. Skincare. A meditation app on his phone, already open.
He begins his routine. Methodical, serious.
Not anxious — this is protocol. He has a system.

[12-14s] The cat jumps onto the bathroom counter. Looks at Tiger's face.
Looks at the mirror. Looks back at Tiger.
The cat has no lines anywhere. The cat is fine.

[14-15s] Tiger: looks at the cat. Back at the mirror.
"Ты не понимаешь. Тебе нечего терять."
STOP.
```

## Промпт 2 — Медитация (неправильная причина, правильный результат)

**Сюжет:** Тигр медитирует. Объясняет коту зачем. Причина неожиданная.

```
Stylized 3D animation, warm family-film aesthetic.
@Image1 as Tiger + black cat. Location: living room, evening light.
9:16 vertical, 15 seconds.

[0-2s] HOOK: Tiger is sitting in perfect meditation pose on his yoga mat.
Eyes closed. Breathing deliberately. The room is calm.
The cat sits nearby watching him with full professional judgment.

[2-5s] One eye opens. Tiger sees the cat watching.
Tiger: "Стресс вызывает кортизол. Кортизол разрушает коллаген."
Cat: stares. 
Tiger: closes eye. Returns to meditation.

[5-9s] Tiger meditating. He's actually good at this — the stillness, 
the breathing, the presence. Whatever his reason, the practice is real.
A few seconds of genuine peace on screen.

[9-11s] Cat: slowly moves closer to the meditation mat.
Sits on the corner of it.
Tiger: both eyes open. Looks at cat. Looks at the mat.
Closes eyes again. Tolerates this.

[11-13s] Tiger: after a moment of real quiet:
"Кортизол снизился. Это заметно на лице?"
He opens his eyes. Genuinely asking.
Cat: one slow blink.

[13-15s] Tiger: interprets this as a yes.
He nods. The practice was productive.
Returns to meditation, slightly more satisfied.
Caption: "5 минут. Каждый день. Ради хорошей кожи."
STOP.
```

## Промпт 3 — Тигр Объясняет Стресс (Нора)

**Сюжет:** Нора жалуется на стресс. Тигр даёт совет — со своей специфической точки зрения.

```
Stylized 3D animation, warm family-film aesthetic.
@Image1 as Tiger + Nora. Location: their usual café.
9:16 vertical, 15 seconds.

[0-2s] HOOK: Nora is visibly stressed. Work, life, everything.
She explains this to Tiger. Tiger is listening — in his focused way.
"Я просто устала. Стресс везде."

[2-5s] Tiger: quiet for a moment. Then:
"Ты понимаешь что происходит с лицом при хроническом стрессе?"
Nora: "..."
Tiger: "Кортизол. Коллаген. Я тебе покажу исследования."

[5-9s] Nora: "Тигр. Я не говорю о своём лице."
Tiger: thinks about this genuinely.
"Хорошо. А о чём говоришь?"
Nora: starts explaining. Tiger: listens. Actually listens.
His paw stops reaching for his phone. He just... sits with it.

[9-12s] After she finishes. Quiet.
Tiger: "Знаешь что мне помогает?"
He shows her his meditation app.
"Не для кожи. Просто — помогает."
He says this slightly gruffly, like it's embarrassing to admit.

[12-14s] Nora: looks at him. Then at the app. Then at him.
"Ты медитируешь."
Tiger: "Я управляю стрессом."
Nora: "...медитируешь."
Tiger: "Управляю стрессом."

[14-15s] Nora: takes his phone. Downloads the app.
Tiger: doesn't say anything. Drinks his coffee.
His tail: one small, quiet curl. That's enough.
STOP.
```

## Промпт 4 — Утренний Ритуал (без слов)

**Сюжет:** 15 секунд утренней wellness-рутины тигра. Красиво, комично, узнаваемо.

```
Stylized 3D animation, warm family-film aesthetic, cinematic quality.
@Image1 as Tiger. Location: apartment — bathroom, kitchen, living room.
9:16 vertical, 15 seconds. SILENT — no dialogue.

[0-3s] Tiger's morning ritual opening: he stands at the mirror.
Full attention. Studies his reflection. Nods once — assessment passed.

[3-6s] Kitchen: Tiger makes tea or coffee. But also: supplements on the counter.
He takes them with the exactness of someone following a protocol.
Everything measured. Nothing casual. It matters.

[6-9s] Living room: 5-minute meditation. He sits.
Breathing. Eyes closed. The apartment is quiet.
The cat walks into frame, sits next to him, also still.
Together, without acknowledgment, they are both just... here.

[9-12s] Tiger opens his wellness/stress app. Reviews last night's data:
sleep quality, stress score, readiness. He studies it.
Makes a small note of something. Adjusts his plan slightly.
This is a system. He runs it every day.

[12-14s] Tiger gets dressed — precise, unhurried.
Checks his reflection one more time. The small line from yesterday:
his expression: almost imperceptibly softer. It's fine.

[14-15s] He leaves. Door closes.
Cat: sits where the meditation mat was.
Looks at the camera.
STOP.
Caption: "Каждый день. Не потому что надо. Потому что заметно."
```

## Промпт 6 — Клиент в Панике (Тигр как нечаянный коуч)

**Сюжет:** Клиент приходит к тигру-профессионалу явно измотанным стрессом. Тигр замечает.

```
Stylized 3D animation, warm family-film aesthetic.
@Image1 as Tiger in professional setting (barber chair context or similar).
9:16 vertical, 15 seconds.

[0-2s] HOOK: Client sits down. They are visibly a mess — stress written everywhere.
Tiger begins his work. Studies the client. Not just the professional task.
Something else catches his attention.

[2-5s] Tiger: works. But glances at the client's face.
Тёмные круги. Зажатые плечи. Попытка улыбнуться.
Tiger: "Плохая неделя?"
Client: "Плохой месяц."

[5-8s] Tiger: continues working. Quiet.
Then, carefully, like sharing classified information:
"Стресс видно на лице раньше чем ты его чувствуешь."
Client: "...правда?"
Tiger: "Кортизол. Я читал исследования."

[8-11s] Tiger: while still working, with the authority of a professional:
gives his stress management protocol. 5 minutes of the app. No screens before bed.
The specific supplement. The one breathing technique.
Client: listens. This was not expected but they're taking notes.

[11-13s] Tiger finishes the work. The client looks at themselves.
Better. Professionally. But also — something else.
Something in how they're sitting is different.

[13-15s] Client: "Вы психолог тоже?"
Tiger: "Я специалист по внешности."
His look: this was 100% about the appearance.
Client: they leave looking genuinely better.
Tiger: back to work. One satisfied tail flick.
STOP.
```
