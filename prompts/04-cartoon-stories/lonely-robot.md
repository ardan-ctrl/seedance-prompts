# Одинокий Робот

**Жанр:** Трогательная история / Sci-Fi  
**Длительность:** 4 сцены × 5s = 20s (или 2 сцены × 15s)  
**Арка:** Одиночество → Удивление → Нежность → Надежда  
**Источник:** glbgpt.com

---

## Промпт (4 сцены, консистентный персонаж)

```
A lonely robot wakes up in an abandoned factory (Scene 1).
It walks outside and sees a sunset wasteland (Scene 2).
It discovers a small flower growing through concrete and gently touches it (Scene 3).
Finally, it looks up and smiles at the sky (Scene 4).

Keep robot appearance consistent throughout all scenes.
Emotional transition from confusion to warmth.
Cinematic camera, warm tones shift from cold to golden, epic mood, no flicker.
```

---

## Расширенная версия (15s, 1 сцена — пробуждение)

```
Cinematic stylized 3D animation. Warm amber-to-rust color palette.

A humanoid robot with aged brushed-steel plating, softly glowing blue eyes, and
visible joint-wear lies dormant in an abandoned industrial factory: concrete floor
cracked, vines growing through broken windows, late afternoon light cutting through
dust in golden shafts.

Its eyes flicker — once, twice — and open. Blue light pulses in its chest.
It slowly lifts one hand, examining the aged metal of its own fingers. It rises
to sitting — movement stiff from long dormancy, servos protesting.
It stands. It looks around the empty factory. It is completely alone.

Camera: starts on ground-level close-up of the eyes flickering, slowly pulls back
to reveal the full scale of the empty factory around the small robot figure.
Final frame: wide shot, robot standing in a shaft of golden light, dust motes
drifting, absolute silence.

Total: 15s / 1 shot / 16:9. Cinematic 3D, warm tones, no dialogue.
```

## Расширенная версия (15s, сцена с цветком)

```
Cinematic stylized 3D animation. Warm golden hour light.

A worn humanoid robot stands in a ruined post-industrial wasteland — collapsed
buildings, scattered debris, grey concrete. It walks slowly, each step deliberate,
its blue eye-glow dimmed by loneliness.

Then — it stops. Looks down. Growing through a crack in the concrete: a single
small yellow wildflower, vibrant and alive against all the grey.

The robot crouches — a slow, careful descent, not to crush the flower.
It raises one metal hand slowly toward the petals. Extreme close-up:
a huge metal finger gently, hesitantly touching the edge of a petal.
The petal does not break.

The robot's blue eyes brighten slightly. It stays crouched, looking at the flower,
for a long moment. Then gently cups both hands around it without touching —
a gesture of protection.

Camera: wide establishing shot → medium follow as it stops → close-up on flower →
extreme close-up on metal finger touching petal → pull back to wide as it cups hands.

Total: 15s / multi-shot / 16:9
```
