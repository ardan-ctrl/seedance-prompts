# Тигр и Нора — Дружба

**Персонажи:** Тигр + Нора  
**Жанр:** Комедия / Тёплая дружба  
**Тон:** Честность без жестокости. Понимание без объяснений.

---

## Кто такая Нора (в промптах)

```
Nora: human woman. Tiger's best friend — no romantic tension, no ambiguity.
Age: similar to Tiger (undefined, adult).
Energy: warm but direct. She says what's true. He listens.
She is the only person who sees behind the arrogance.
She knows him well enough to finish his sentences.
He trusts her completely, shows it through attention not performance.
Appearance: real, approachable, sardonic smile, watchful eyes.
NOT: a love interest. NOT: a sidekick. Equal.
```

## Промпт 1 — Нора Говорит Правду

```
Stylized 3D animation, warm family-film aesthetic.
@Image1 Tiger + Nora (human woman, warm and direct).
Setting: familiar comfortable space — her kitchen, his empty salon, usual café.
9:16 vertical, 12 seconds. No professional context — just two people talking.

[0-2s] HOOK: Tiger is mid-story. Telling Nora something complicated about a situation.
His hands are explaining. He's being slightly dramatic.
This is the Tiger only Nora gets to see.

[2-4s] Nora: listening with the face of someone who already knows the end of this story.
But she lets him finish. That's the friendship.

[4-8s] Tiger: finishes. "Ну что скажешь?"

"Ты снова сделал всё правильно и почему-то расстроен."

Nora: "Это именно так."
Tiger: [pause] "...Это немного так."

[8-10s] Nora: "Ты знаешь что сделать?"

Nora: "Ты это сделаешь?"

Nora: already nodding. She knew this answer too.

[10-12s] Tiger: reaches for his coffee. Stares into it.
"Иногда ты раздражаешь."
Nora: "Иногда ты тоже."
Tiger: [smallest smile — rare, real] 
They drink their coffee.
Caption: "Некоторые разговоры не нужно заканчивать."
```

## Промпт 2 — Нора Просит Помощи (Тигр не может помочь)

```
[0-2s] HOOK: For once: Nora is the one with the problem.
Tiger is listening. This is unusual for him — he's good at talking, good at fixing.
He is trying very hard to just listen.

[2-4s] Nora explains. Tiger listens. 
His paw moves once to offer advice. He pulls it back.
Listens more.
He wants to solve this. There is no solving this.

[4-8s] Tiger: "Хочешь, я..."
Nora: "Нет. Просто сиди."
Tiger: sits.
This is one of the hardest things for him.
He sits with it. With her. Says nothing.
His tail is very still.

[8-10s] Nora: eventually. "Спасибо."
Tiger: "Я ничего не сделал."
Nora: "Я знаю. Именно поэтому — спасибо."
Tiger: doesn't fully understand this. But he accepts it.

[10-12s] Tiger: "Ты в порядке?"
Nora: "Буду."
Tiger: nods once. Good.
They stay in the comfortable quiet.
Caption: "Самое сложное — это не делать."
```

## Промпт 3 — Нора Встречает Очередную Женщину Тигра

```
[0-2s] HOOK: Tiger walks in with someone — a new person.
She is beautiful. He is slightly more careful than usual.
He introduces: "Это Нора. Мой друг."
Nora: extends a hand. Genuine smile. "Много слышала."

[2-4s] The new person glances at Nora. Then at Tiger.
Then back at Nora: "Вы давно знакомы?"
Nora: "Достаточно давно."
Tiger: feels something in this exchange but cannot identify it.

[4-8s] They talk. Tiger is in the middle: slightly nervous (rare), managing two conversations.
The cat appears from nowhere. Sits with Nora immediately.
Tiger: "Ты его видишь первый раз."
Nora: already petting the cat. "Он разбирается в людях."
Cat: green eyes on the new person. Assessing. Slow.

[8-10s] The new person excuses herself for a moment.
Tiger: turns to Nora. Instant: "Что думаешь?"
Nora: "Ты уверен, что хочешь знать?"
Tiger: [pause] "Нет."
Nora: "Тогда не спрашивай."

[10-12s] Tiger looks toward where the person went. Back at Nora.
Nora: takes a sip of her drink. Says nothing.
Cat: still watching the door.
Caption: "Нора всегда знает."
```
