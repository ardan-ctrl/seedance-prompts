# Тигр и Кот — Молчаливая Конкуренция

**Персонажи:** Тигр + Чёрный Кот  
**Жанр:** Комедия отношений / Без слов  
**Тон:** Deadpan. Никто не говорит о войне. Война существует.

---

## Промпт 1 — Кот Получает Комплимент Тигра

```
Stylized 3D animation, warm family-film aesthetic, expressive anthropomorphic character design.
Warm home/salon interior lighting, shallow depth of field.
@Image1 Tiger: slim anthropomorphic tiger, home casual clothes, half-lidded.
Black Kitten: small fluffy, vivid green eyes, blue moon-charm collar, upright tail.
9:16 vertical, 12 seconds.

[0-2s] HOOK: Tiger and the cat are both sitting in the same room.
A woman visits. She greets Tiger. Then she sees the cat.
She lights up completely.
"О, какой красивый котик!"
She reaches down and pets the cat.
Tiger stands slightly behind. Watching.

[2-4s] Tiger's expression: his default. Arms loosely crossed.
Nothing visibly changes.
Internally: the tail makes one slow swish.
The woman: fully focused on the cat now. "Он такой... элегантный."
The cat: sits perfectly, tail curled, not acknowledging Tiger at all.

[4-8s] Tiger: smoothly steps forward. Does not interrupt the petting.
He simply exists in the frame more.
The woman: glances up at Tiger. "И ты тоже красивый!"
She resumes petting the cat.
Tiger: [long beat] "Спасибо."
His tail does not move.

[8-10s] Eventually: the woman leaves. Tiger and cat are alone.
Tiger looks at the cat.
Cat looks at Tiger.
Tiger: says nothing.
Cat: blinks once. Slowly. The blink of absolute victory.

[10-12s] Tiger turns away. Straightens something on his desk.
His tail — very briefly — flicks once.
The cat curls up and closes its eyes.
Caption: "Продолжается."
```

## Промпт 2 — Кот Чинит То, Что Сломал Тигр

```
[0-2s] HOOK: Tiger is working on something (in his space, alone).
He tries to fix something — a shelf, a tool, a piece of equipment.
It... gets worse.
He tries again. More worse.
He steps back. Assesses the situation.

[2-4s] The cat walks in. Sees the situation.
Stops. Surveys the damage.
Looks at Tiger.
Tiger: "Я знаю."
Cat: approaches the problem. Paws at it once. Twice.
Then: with one small deliberate push — it's fixed.

[4-8s] Tiger stares at it. The thing that defeated him.
Fixed. By the cat. In 4 seconds.
He looks at the cat.
The cat has already walked away.
Is now sitting on the windowsill, tail curled, looking outside.
As if nothing happened.

[8-10s] Tiger: looks at the fixed object. Looks at the cat's back.
"Ты сделал это намеренно."
Cat: doesn't turn around.

[10-12s] Tiger: sits down. Opens his phone. Pretends to scroll.
Cat: turns around just enough to check that Tiger is watching.
Then turns back to the window.
Caption: "Живут вместе три года."
```

## Промпт 3 — Конкуренция за Внимание (Самый вирусный)

```
[0-2s] HOOK: Tiger is doing something impressive — a skill demonstration
or careful, precise work. He's being watched by someone (client, woman, viewer).
He knows he's being watched. He performs slightly.

[2-4s] The cat walks into frame. 
It does one extremely simple thing.
Falls asleep on a piece of fabric nearby. In the light. Just... perfectly. Like art.
Everyone in the room: looks at the cat.

[4-8s] Tiger continues his impressive work.
Is anyone watching? He glances sideways.
All eyes: on the sleeping cat.
Tiger sets down his tools.

[8-10s] Tiger: "Он просто спит."
Observer: "Но так мило!"
Tiger: [long look] "Я только что сделал [профессиональное достижение]."
Observer: "Да, да, очень хорошо. Но посмотри какой он маленький!"

[10-12s] Tiger picks up his tools again.
Gets back to work.
In the background: the cat opens one green eye.
Looks directly at camera.
Closes it again.
Caption: "Всё по плану."
```

## Промпт 4 — Утренняя Смена Позиций

```
[0-2s] HOOK: Morning. Tiger's apartment.
There's only one good spot: the armchair by the window. Perfect morning light.
Tiger walks toward it with his coffee.
The cat is already in it.

[2-4s] Tiger looks at the chair. Looks at the cat.
The cat: facing away. Looking out the window.
Doesn't acknowledge Tiger's existence.
Tiger: sits on the couch. It's fine.

[4-8s] Tigerdrinks his coffee on the couch.
The cat shifts in the armchair. Stretches. Gets even more comfortable.
Tiger watches this.
Tiger: "Ты знал, что я иду."
Cat: doesn't respond. Obviously knew.

[8-10s] Tiger stands up. Goes to make another coffee.
Returns. The cat has moved to the couch.
The armchair is empty.
Tiger stares at the armchair. Stares at the cat on the couch.

[10-12s] Tiger sits in the armchair.
The moment he sits down: the cat jumps off the couch.
Walks to a third location Tiger hadn't considered. Sits there. Perfect spot.
Tiger is now in the armchair with his coffee. It was never about the armchair.
Caption: "Обычное утро."
```
