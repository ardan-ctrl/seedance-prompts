# Тигр и Любовь — Единственная Слабость

**Персонажи:** Тигр (один или с Норой)  
**Жанр:** Комедия с горчинкой / Эмоциональный якорь  
**Тон:** Не жалость. Самоирония. Достоинство при поражении.

---

## Промпт 1 — "Каждый Раз"

```
Stylized 3D animation, warm family-film aesthetic.
@Image1 Tiger: slim anthropomorphic tiger, professional outfit (after work), half-lidded eyes.
Evening, warm urban interior — bar/café or his apartment.
9:16 vertical, 12 seconds.

[0-2s] HOOK: Tiger sits at a bar counter. Looking at his phone.
We see the last message in the conversation. She: "Ты очень... особенный."
Tiger: stares at this for a long moment. 
He knows what "особенный" means in this context.

[2-4s] He sets the phone face-down on the counter.
Picks up his drink. Holds it.
Stares at the middle distance.
One moment of being just... a person who is tired of a specific thing.

[4-8s] The bartender (anthropomorphic animal — owl, perhaps): 
"Что-то случилось?"
Tiger: "Нет. Всё нормально."
Bartender: looks at the face-down phone. Back at Tiger. 
"Ага."
Silence. Tiger takes a sip.

[8-10s] Tiger picks up the phone. Reads the message once more.
His tail makes one small, resigned movement.
He types three dots. Deletes them. Types them again. Sends.
His reply: "Я знаю :)"

[10-12s] He sets the phone down. Straightens. Looks at himself in the bar mirror.
He looks exactly as put-together as always.
He nods at himself in the mirror: once.
Picks up his jacket. Leaves. Still dignified. Always dignified.
Caption: "Следующий."
```

## Промпт 2 — Тигр и Нора

```
Stylized 3D animation, warm family-film aesthetic.
Characters: @Image1 Tiger + Nora (human woman, warm and direct, tiger's best friend).
They are in a comfortable familiar space — her kitchen, his salon after hours, a usual café.
9:16 vertical, 12 seconds.

[0-2s] HOOK: Tiger is telling Nora about a situation.
We don't hear the words. We see: he's explaining with his hands.
His expression: the resigned one, but performed slightly, because it's Nora.
He can be slightly dramatic with Nora.

[2-4s] Nora listens. She has the face of someone who has heard this story before.
Not because it's the same woman. Because it's the same story.
Tiger finishes. Spreads his hands: "Ну?"
Nora: takes a sip of her coffee. Carefully.

[4-8s] Nora: "Ты снова работал вместо того чтобы просто быть рядом."
Tiger: "Я БЫЛ рядом."
Nora: "Ты исправлял её проблему."
Tiger: [pause] "...Я помог."
Nora: "Она хотела не помощи."
Tiger stares at her.

[8-10s] Long beat. Tiger looks at his coffee.
He knows Nora is right. He always knows Nora is right.
He doesn't say this out loud.

[10-12s] Tiger: "Следующий раз буду просто..."
Nora: already smiling slightly. "Ты скажешь это следующий раз тоже."
Tiger: [small exhale through nose — his version of laughing at himself]
They sit in comfortable silence.
Caption: "Лучший диагноз — тот, что не нужно объяснять."
```

## Промпт 3 — Она Пришла Снова (Клиент)

```
[0-2s] HOOK: A beautiful woman walks into Tiger's salon/clinic.
She's been here before. They know each other.
There is clearly something between them — or was, or almost was.
Tiger: sets down his tools. Straightens.
"Снова?"
She: "Снова."

[2-4s] She sits in his chair. He approaches professionally.
This is professional. He is a professional.
His hands are completely steady.
His tail: one small movement. He controls it.

[4-8s] He works. Perfectly. She watches him in the mirror.
He focuses on the work.
The cat: walks in from nowhere. Sits between them. Looks back and forth.
Tiger: "Уйди."
Cat: does not leave. Actually sits down more firmly.

[8-10s] She: looks at the cat. Smiles.
"Он у тебя давно?"
Tiger: "Три года. Он не мой. Он сам так решил."
She: laughs. Easy laugh. The kind that's the problem.
Tiger: in the mirror, his expression: exactly the "every time" face.

[10-12s] The work is done. She looks perfect.
She: looks at him in the mirror. One beat.
"Ты когда-нибудь отдыхаешь?"
Tiger: "Нет."
She leaves. The door closes.
Cat: stares at the door. Looks at Tiger.
Tiger: "Не начинай."
```

## Промпт 4 — Love Fail как Хук (самый короткий формат)

```
[0-2s] HOOK: Tiger sits on a park bench. Evening.
Two seats. Just him.
Someone was going to be here.
The cat appears and sits in the empty spot.

[2-6s] Tiger looks at the cat.
The cat looks forward. City lights ahead.
Tiger: "Ты не то, кого я ждал."
Cat: blinks slowly. 
Tiger: [long exhale] "Хотя..."

[6-12s] They sit together watching the city.
In silence. Comfortable, resigned, genuine.
The cat's tail wraps around Tiger's wrist slightly.
He doesn't shake it off.
Caption: "Вечер не пропал."
```
