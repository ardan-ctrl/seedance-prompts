# Tiger — Bad at Sports (Comedy Arc)

> Тигр не атлетичен. Он это знает. Он никогда этого не признает.

---

## Промпт 1 — Первый Push-Up

**Сюжет:** Тигр делает первое отжимание в своей жизни. Одно. Встаёт. Объявляет победу.

```
Stylized 3D animation, warm family-film aesthetic, adult situational humor.
@Image1 as Tiger. Tiger in sleek athletic wear — tiger stripe pattern, fitness tracker on wrist.
9:16 vertical, 15 seconds. Location: clean minimal home gym or living room floor.

[0-2s] HOOK: Tiger stands over a yoga mat with the posture of someone about to conquer
something significant. Full concentration. He adjusts his position carefully.
The cat is on the couch. Watching. Green eyes unblinking.

[2-5s] Tiger slowly lowers himself into push-up position. This takes longer than expected.
His arms are shaking from the effort of lowering. He hasn't started yet.

[5-10s] The push-up. One. Full. Push-up.
His face: concentration of a surgeon defusing a bomb.
Halfway down: genuine struggle. His form is questionable. He commits.
He makes it back up.

[10-12s] Tiger stands. Dusts his paws off. Slow satisfied nod.
Checks his fitness tracker. The number: 1.
He nods again. Good session.

[12-14s] Tiger walks past the cat. "Хорошая работа."
Cat: does not move. Green eyes follow him.

[14-15s] STINGER: Tiger's phone buzzes — fitness app notification:
"First push-up complete! 99 more to reach your goal."
Tiger looks at the screen. Looks at the camera. Slowly puts phone face-down.
STOP.
```

## Промпт 2 — Пробежка (50 метров)

**Сюжет:** Тигр выходит на утреннюю пробежку с энергией спортсмена. Возвращается через 45 секунд.

```
Stylized 3D animation, warm family-film aesthetic.
@Image1 as Tiger. Outdoor setting: clean urban street, morning light.
9:16 vertical, 15 seconds.

[0-2s] HOOK: Tiger exits his building in full running gear.
Wireless earbuds, water bottle, expensive sneakers.
He stretches one leg. Surveys the street. The horizon.
He starts to run.

[2-7s] Running montage: first 5 seconds — Tiger looks incredible.
Strong stride, proper form apparently, city blurring behind him.
He looks like someone who runs marathons.

[7-9s] Second 5 seconds: subtle signs of strain appearing.
Breathing heavier. Stride shortening. The music in his ears has
become very motivating and very necessary.

[9-12s] Tiger slows to a walk. Then stops.
Checks his watch: 50 meters. 45 seconds.
He looks at this data thoughtfully. Like a scientist reviewing results.

[12-14s] He turns around and walks home with the unhurried dignity of
someone who completed exactly what they came to do.

[14-15s] Tiger passes a dog walking with his human.
Dog: jogging effortlessly.
Tiger: does not acknowledge the dog.
His tail: slightly lower than usual.
STOP.
```

## Промпт 3 — Тренажёрный зал

**Сюжет:** Тигр в зале. Все вокруг подтягиваются, жмут, бегут. Тигр ищет самый лёгкий вес.

```
Stylized 3D animation, warm family-film aesthetic.
@Image1 as Tiger. Location: modern gym, human gym-goers in background.
9:16 vertical, 15 seconds.

[0-2s] HOOK: Busy gym. A large muscular man is benching enormous weights on one side.
A woman is sprinting on a treadmill at serious pace. Tiger enters.
He surveys the room with the expression of someone who owns the place.

[2-5s] Tiger approaches the weight rack. Studies it carefully.
He selects a weight. The lightest weight in the rack.
He lifts it. His face: total focus. His form: surprisingly serious for the weight.

[5-9s] MONTAGE: Tiger doing his workout. Every exercise performed with
maximum seriousness and minimum weight. He checks his reflection between sets.
He nods. Good form.

[9-12s] The muscular man walks past. Glances at Tiger's weights.
Man's face: genuinely trying not to react.
Tiger: catches the look. Raises one eyebrow.
Adds another small weight. "Прогрессивная нагрузка."

[12-14s] Tiger finishes. Towels off. Drinks from his water bottle.
Looks at himself in the gym mirror with full satisfaction.

[14-15s] Behind him in the mirror: the cat.
Tiger turns around. The cat is in the gym. On the pull-up bar.
Hanging. Effortlessly.
STOP. — How did the cat get here?
```

## Промпт 4 — Тигр vs Кот: Кто Атлетичнее

**Сюжет:** Молчаливое соревнование. Кот выигрывает без усилий. Тигр не принимает поражение.

```
Stylized 3D animation, warm family-film aesthetic.
@Image1 as Tiger + black cat. Location: home, living room.
9:16 vertical, 15 seconds. Silent competition — no words needed.

[0-2s] HOOK: Tiger is doing stretches on his yoga mat.
The cat walks into the frame. Sits next to the mat.
Tiger doesn't acknowledge him. The competition has already begun.

[2-5s] Tiger: attempts a stretch. Gets about 60% of the way there.
Cat: without looking at Tiger, stretches to impossible angles.
Completely casually. As if reaching for something.
Tiger: sees this. Says nothing. Stretches harder.

[5-8s] Tiger: attempts a balance pose. Wobbles. Holds it. Barely.
Cat: sits on one paw. Perfectly balanced. Licks the other paw.
Not a competition. Just existing.

[8-11s] Tiger attempts something more ambitious — a full split.
Gets about 40% of the way down. His face: everything is fine.
Cat: from his sitting position, extends one leg completely flat.
Still licking his paw.

[11-13s] Tiger stands. Dusts off. 
"Хорошая тренировка." To no one.
Rolls up his yoga mat with great dignity.

[13-15s] Tiger walks out of frame.
Cat: watches him go. Returns to sitting normally.
One blink.
STOP.
```

## Промпт 6 — После Тренировки (Love Fail вариант)

**Сюжет:** Тигр приходит домой после "тренировки". Встречает кого-то кто действительно занимается спортом. Love fail через атлетику.

```
Stylized 3D animation, warm family-film aesthetic.
@Image1 as Tiger + a human woman (athletic, actually fit).
Location: building entrance/lobby. 15 seconds.

[0-2s] HOOK: Tiger arrives home after his run. He looks like he ran 10km.
(He ran 50 meters 20 minutes ago and walked very slowly back.)
He's sweaty, tired, satisfied. Opens the building door.

[2-5s] In the lobby: someone is ACTUALLY finishing a real run.
Fast, efficient, actually athletic. They remove earbuds. 
See Tiger. Smile.
"Good run?"

[5-8s] Tiger: adjusts his posture immediately.
"Да. Хорошая работа сегодня."
Them: "How far did you go?"
Tiger: pause. "Нужное расстояние."

[8-11s] They laugh. Good-naturedly. Tiger takes this as approval.
They head toward the elevator together.
Casual conversation. Tiger: trying to walk normally (his legs are tired).

[11-13s] Elevator arrives. They step in together.
Tiger: "Вы тоже следите за собой."
Them: "I just qualified for a half-marathon, actually."
Tiger: "..."

[13-15s] Elevator doors close. Just Tiger.
He looks at his fitness tracker.
50 meters. 45 seconds.
His tail droops exactly 2 centimeters.
STOP.
```
